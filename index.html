GRAND OLD PARR COLLABORATION

Grand Old Parr often simply just Old Parr is a blended Scotch whisky named after the reputed oldest man in England. Launched in 1909, it is found in export markets such as Japan, Mexico, South America (specially Colombia) and the United States, and is no longer distributed in the United Kingdom. It is owned, distilled and distributed by Diageo, the British drinks company.

We are pleased to present our new collaboration with Gran old Parr. This new whisky has a golden color view. The nose is clean, light and fresh, with a touch of green leaves and flowers and hints of oak and freshly stirred earth. On the palate, the taste of ripe fruit, peaches and raspberries. It has a clean and smooth finish.
